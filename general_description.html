General Description of Basketball

Basketball is a team sport where two teams of five players each try to score by shooting a ball through the other team's hoop. Basketball is played on a rectangular floor called the court that has a hoop at each end. Hoops are typically 10 feet off the ground, although some leagues for young children play on shorter hoops that may be 8-9 feet tall. Just like there are different kinds of basketball hoops, there are also several types of basketballs and court surfaces. More information on that can be found here. Players on offense advance the ball down the court by bouncing it up and down while walking or running. This is called dribbling. They can also pass the ball to their teammates. A variety of shots including the lay-up, the jump-shot, and the dunk can be used to score points for your team. A field goal is worth two points, unless it's shot from behind the three-point-line, in which case it is worth three points.



Now, 140 years later, basketball has become of the most popular sports in the world, played and watched by milions of people across the globe.
